  1. Log out and log back in
  2. Run: docker ps
  3. Should work without manual start

To Disable:
  Delete: C:\Users\USER\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\
          Start Menu\Programs\Startup\Docker-Startup.lnk

To Re-enable:
  pwsh> .\setup-startup-folder.ps1
